# International rules that need to be maintained

1. **ISO/IEC 27001 (Information Security)** manages risks and data encryption to prove a company handles data safely.
2. **SOC 2 Type II (Cloud Security)** focuses on security, availability, processing integrity, and privacy for B2B trust.
3. **GDPR (EU Privacy)** covers the right to be forgotten and data portability as a legal requirement for international markets.
4. **ISO/IEC 27701 (Privacy Information)** extends security to specifically manage personal data and PII.
5. **OWASP ASVS (Application Verification)** provides a technical framework to ensure code is protected against modern hacking.
6. **ISO 9001 (Quality Management)** ensures consistency in product quality and professional development processes.
7. **ISO 9241-210 (Human-Centered Design)** certifies an iterative process focused on building software for humans.
8. **ISO 9241-11 (Usability)** sets the global definition for effectiveness, efficiency, and user satisfaction.
9. **ISO 25010 (Software Quality)** provides a blueprint for reliability, performance, maintainability, and portability.
10. **ISO/IEC 40500 / WCAG 2.2 (Digital Accessibility)** makes apps perceivable and operable for blind and colorblind users globally.
11. **Section 508 (USA Accessibility)** is required for software used by US government-related entities.
12. **EN 301 549 (EU Public Sector)** serves as a digital accessibility passport for the European market.
13. **GIGW (India Guidelines)** ensures integration readiness with Indian Digital Public Infrastructure.
14. **ISO/IEC 12207 (Software Life Cycle)** defines professional standards for how code is written, tested, and retired.
15. **IEEE 730 (Software Quality Assurance)** ensures that testing phases are effective and meet global quality plans.
16. **IEEE 7000 (Ethical Design)** addresses ethical concerns and prevents hidden biases or dark patterns in system design.
17. **ISO 14001 (Sustainability)** focuses on reducing the carbon footprint of servers to ensure software is environmentally green.
18. **PCI DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard)**: Essential if the platform handles payments for ads, subscriptions, or marketplace transactions.
19. **COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)**: Mandatory for protecting children's privacy and data on social platforms (USA).
20. **EU Digital Services Act (DSA)**: Critical for modern social media, covering content moderation, transparency, and illegal content management.
21. **ISO/IEC 27017 & 27018 (Cloud Security & PII)**: Specialized standards for security controls and protecting personally identifiable information (PII) specifically in cloud environments.
22. **CCPA/CPRA (California Consumer Privacy Act)**: Modern privacy requirements for users in the US market, complementing GDPR.
23. **NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF)**: Provides a high-level taxonomy of cybersecurity outcomes and a methodology to assess and manage those outcomes.
24. **ISO/IEC 38500 (IT Governance)**: Ensures that the platform's IT infrastructure and management align with legal, regulatory, and ethical obligations.
