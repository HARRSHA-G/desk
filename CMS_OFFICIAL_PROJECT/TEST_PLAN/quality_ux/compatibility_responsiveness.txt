==============================
COMPATIBILITY & RESPONSIVENESS
==============================

Scope
-----
- Cross-browser/device/OS coverage (desktop/mobile/tablet), high-DPI, and light/dark themes.

Tooling
-------
- Playwright/Cypress for viewport/browsers; BrowserStack/Sauce for wider OS coverage.

Checks
------
- Layout integrity at breakpoints, touch/mouse parity, text scaling, and RTL readiness if needed.

Theming
-------
- Verify both light/dark palettes and contrast; ensure assets/icons render correctly on high-DPI.

Automation
----------
- Run scripted viewport sweeps in CI; maintain a matrix of priority browsers/devices.
